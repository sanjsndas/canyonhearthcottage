Your privacy matters.

We believe in transparency, simplicity, and respect for your personal data. This policy outlines how Hearth & Ember collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: June 2026 privacy@hearthandember.com

1. Information we collect

We collect minimal data to provide a personalised experience. This includes:

  • Contact information — name, email address (when you subscribe or send a message).
  • Usage data — pages visited, interactions (via cookies, see our Cookies Policy).
  • Newsletter engagement — opens, clicks, and preferences.

2. How we use your data

  • To send you the Hearth & Ember newsletter and updates you’ve requested.
  • To respond to your messages, recipe questions, or collaboration inquiries.
  • To improve our content and website experience.

We never sell your personal data to third parties. Ever.

Our promise: We treat your data like a family recipe — with care, respect, and confidentiality.

3. Data storage & security

Your information is stored on secure servers with restricted access. We use industry-standard encryption (SSL) for data transmission. We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the services you’ve requested, or as required by law.

4. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for email communications at any time.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@hearthandember.com. We’ll respond within 30 days.

5. Third-party services

We use trusted services to run our website and newsletter:

  • Email platform: We use a reputable email service to manage newsletters. They process data only on our behalf.
  • Analytics: We use privacy-friendly analytics to understand page traffic — no personal identifiers are stored.

6. Children’s privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We’ll post changes on this page and, if significant, notify you via email or a notice on the site.
