    Frequently asked questions

    How do I adjust a recipe for high altitude?

    Reduce leavening by 20%, increase liquid slightly, and extend baking time. We have a full guide in our resource hub.

    What’s the best way to store fresh herbs?

    Treat them like flowers: trim stems, place in water, cover loosely with a bag, and refrigerate. For hardy herbs, wrap in damp paper.

    Why did my sourdough not rise?

    Usually due to weak starter or under-proofing. Check your starter activity and ambient temperature.
