Our story

Hearth & Ember was born from a love of slow cooking, honest ingredients, and the quiet wisdom passed down through generations. We are cooks, writers, and gardeners who believe that the best meals tell a story.

Founded 2024 100% ad-free 12k+ happy readers

The hearth philosophy

We don’t follow trends — we follow the seasons. Every recipe on Hearth & Ember is tested at least a dozen times in our own kitchens. We prioritise flavour, nutrition, and the joy of cooking over spectacle.

Our team of four (plus a rotating cast of guest contributors) brings together pastry chefs, fermentation nerds, and foragers. We share what we’ve learned from cookbooks, grandmothers, and happy accidents.

“Cooking is not about perfection — it’s about presence. We want you to feel that warmth.”

Our values

Seasonal & whole-food — we cook with what nature provides.
Technique first — master the basics, then improvise.
No waste — from root to stem, we use everything.
