Our cookie philosophy.

Just like a perfectly baked cookie, we keep it simple, transparent, and satisfying. Here’s how we use cookies to make your experience better.

Last updated: June 2026 privacy@hearthandember.com

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, improve site performance, and understand how you interact with our content. We use them to make your visit feel warm and welcoming — like a good cup of tea.

2. How we use cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly (e.g., navigation, security).
  • Preference cookies: They remember your settings (like language or region).
  • Analytics cookies: We use privacy-friendly analytics to count visits and see which pages are most loved. No personal data is shared.
  • Newsletter cookies: They help us understand if our emails are useful and engaging.

Your choice: You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. However, some parts of our site may not work as intended without them.

3. Third-party cookies

We use a few trusted third-party services that may place cookies:

  • Email platform: Our newsletter service uses cookies to track engagement and improve deliverability.
  • Analytics: We use a privacy-conscious analytics tool (no personal data collected).

These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we only work with those who respect user privacy.

4. Managing cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through settings. You can block all cookies, delete existing ones, or set alerts before they are placed. For detailed instructions, visit your browser’s help section.

If you prefer, you can opt out of non-essential cookies by adjusting your preferences. We respect your choice.

5. Updates to this policy

We may update this Cookies Policy occasionally to reflect changes in technology or regulations. Any updates will be posted here with a revised date.

If you have questions, please reach out at privacy@hearthandember.com.
